Tutorial

The RC car is controlled using an on android via Bluetooth, with integration of many sensors making it capable of:

  • Measuring ambient temperature and adjusting the temperature inside car using an Ac system accordingly.
  • Adjustable the intensity of head/ backlights according to the lighting conditions of the environment.
  • Detecting a light source and following it via a light sensor.
  • Following a line drawn on the ground beneath it.
  • Detecting a nearby fire hazard and issuing a warning.
  • Avoid nearby obstacles using an ultrasonic sensor.
  • Detecting the vibration of the car and issuing a warning in highly vibration.
  • Detecting the slope of the car and issuing a warning in highly vibration.
  • Detecting the L.B and make indication.
  • Detect a movable object issuing a horn.
  • The car moves when detecting sound.

Sensors

  • Displaying temperature and battery’s voltage
    • design

      • Protus file here

    • Temperature

      • 1-Target:
      A) display the temperature in Celsius on LCD
      B) if the temperature is more than 40 C the fan will be turned on and turned off if the temperature is less than 35

    • Battery’s voltage

      • 1-Target
      A) Display the battery’s voltage on LCD
      B) Indicate the low battery
      2- Sensor :
      Voltage divider “ the Arduino reads one over three of battery voltage and the the code will multiply it by 3 then display the result ” “in our case we use 12v battery “

  • Vibration sensor :

    • vibrations is considered its input and it converts this input into voltage as an output which is put into a led .
    This sensor can be used in the vehicles which carry glass and ceramic to alarm the driver to drive slowly in order to protect the cargo from destroying

  • Flame sensor :

    • -Detects a flame or a light source of a wavelength in the range of 760nm-1100 nm.
    -Detection range: up to 100 cm.
    -Adjustable detection range.
    -Detection angle about 60 degrees, it is sensitive to the flame spectrum.

  • PIR sensor

    • Motion Detector Sensor, it detects the motion of a human or an animal ,it's used as warnning that someone is passing in front of the car so a buzzer is used to warn the driver

  • Line follower sensor

    • The line follower sensor gives the car the ability to detect lines by detecting the light coming from its own infrared LED
    When light fall on a white surface it is almost full reflected and in case of black surface light is completely absorbed

  • LDR sensor

    • Using the dependency of the LDR on light, as the light intensity changes, the resistance of the LDR changes, and that can be translated into controlling the lights of the car depending on the lighting condition of the environment.

  • Light follower sensor

    • This module allows you to detect when sound has exceeded a set point you select. Sound is detected via a microphone and fed into an LM393 op amp.
    The sound level set point is adjusted via an on board potentiometer. When the sound level exceeds the set point, an LED on the module is illuminated and the output is sent low.

  • Tilt sensor

    • It can detect when it is tilted,contains a small metal ball that will complete a circuit depending on where it is in the sensor. Because the sensor is very basic,
    it can only detect large changes in its tilt, and cannot measure the angle of its tilt.
